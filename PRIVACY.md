# Privacy Policy

Code Web Chat is **privacy-first**. We adhere to a **strict zero telemetry policy**.

**No Data Collection** \
The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any telemetry data.

**Local Operation** \
All operations are performed locally on your machine.

**Direct Communication** \
Prompts are sent directly from your browser to the chatbots or from your editor to the model providers you configure.
